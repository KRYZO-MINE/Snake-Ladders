      📖 How to Play

      🎯 Objective

      Be the first player to reach square 100!

      🎲 Rules

      • Player 1 starts first
      • Players take turns rolling the dice
      • Roll a 6 and get an extra turn!
      • Need an exact roll to reach 100

      🪜 Ladders

      Land on a ladder to climb up quickly!

      🐍 Snakes

      Watch out for snakes - they'll slide you down!

      ⌨️ Shortcuts

      • Space - Roll Dice
      • M - Toggle Sound
      • R - Restart Game
      • I - Toggle Instructions
      • Escape - Close Modals
      • 2/3/4 - Select Number of Players
      • Enter - Start Game
